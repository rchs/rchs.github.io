Backend Developer

COMPANY DESCRIPTION

Diagnal is an electronic medical record system loved by both doctors and patients alike. We make technology less painful for doctors and health records more accessible to patients.

Diagnal is also part of roaches, a group of technology companies that challenges the status quo across multiple verticals.

Personal Traits

  • You are very comfortable with abstractions

    You can see beyond the lines of code. If that didn’t make sense, maybe you should stop reading. But if you are the kind that has no problem visualizing and transforming complicated relationships in your head, chances are we'll hit it off.

  • You can see the future

    When you write, you anticipate the kinds of issues that might come up two years from now, and you make sure that the code will easily weather those issues.

  • Confident and Explorative at the same time

    When you say you know something what you usually mean is that you have gone 5 level deeper than any other person around you. Hence, for things you know you don’t really care about what others say, knowing full well that your way is the best way. But at the same time, you can also get super explorative when it comes to figuring out new ways to solve problems. Long story short, if people in your team call you quietly brilliant, we really want to talk to you.

Work Responsibility

  • Build the Database

    You will be working closing with the founding team and technical leads to build a database that will work with billions of records yet respond to our applications within a millisecond.

  • Build the backend

    You will be responsible for developing and maintaining the application/api server that can scale horizontally, respond at light speed, communicate with different subsystems, is highly fault tolerant, and is also easy on the bills.

  • Code Reviews

    You will coordinate with the Tech Lead to assess the quality of the backend implementations, especially with regards to the quality of the code.

Skill Set

  • Experience working with large data sets
  • Can communicate well in Structured Query Language
  • Node / Python
  • Redis / Memcached
  • Understanding of AWS architecture
