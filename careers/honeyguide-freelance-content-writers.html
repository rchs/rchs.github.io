COMPANY DESCRIPTION

HoneyGuide is an online travel agency specializing in mountain travel. We make sure that men and mountains meet more often. (Of course women too, but the rhyme was too good to pass.)

HoneyGuide is also part of roaches, a group of technology companies that challenges the status quo across multiple verticals.

Visit Website

As content creators, you will help trekkers plan and run their treks on their own. Since that is at the core of what HoneyGuide does, it is super important to put out well researched and actionable content. Hence, the rules of writing for HoneyGuide are:

  • Rule #1: As an independent traveler, if you find the material that you have written actionable and enjoyable, you are done.
  • Rule #2: There will be no more rules.

Personal Traits

  • Must Love Mountains

    If you find yourself looking longingly at the mountains whenever the weather clears and escaping into them whenever the opportunity presents, Keep reading!

  • Skepticism

    The mountains are full of tales as tall as the mountains themselves. You know better than to take everything at face value. If statements like, 'Nepalis are such friendly people' and 'Nepal is such a Shangri La' doesn’t make you cringe, we recommend you not read any further.

  • Hate Boring with a passion

    The usual way to do things bore you to death and you always look for ways to add that extra in whatever you do. The result is that you can’t help but stand out!

Work Responsibility

  • Write

    You will write informative and highly actionable long-form articles. If a trekker feels inspired and empowered enough to take on the mountains after reading what you have written, you got it!!

  • Research

    The kind of content HoneyGuide requires needs quite a bit of research. We hope you enjoy digging through dry books to find that one fantastic insight or call up people on the ground to confirm if it takes 3 hours to reach Sing Gompa.

Skill Set

  • Voracious Reader

    You can be a hundred percent sure that we will ask you what books you have read in the last month or so in the interview. :) We feel that great conversations and even better writing come out only when you couple traveling with reading.

  • Mad Writing Skills

    In your writing samples, we want to see if you can make us feel what you felt while writing the piece. Or, if you are writing an informative article, we want to know the depth of research you undertook to make it actionable. Obviously, a clear and concise writing style and a good understanding of conventions of English is a must.

  • Photography

    No article is complete without pictures that compliment the point being made. We will look for an eye for beauty in the images that you send us.
