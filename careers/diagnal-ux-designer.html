UX Designer

Personal Traits

  • You have gone beyond aesthetics and engineering to realize that it is all about psychological manipulation

    We have all read The Design of Everyday Things and Emotional Design, and we get it. But at the core of both the approaches is an understanding of the human psyche. And your one true passion is to uncover the layers that make up the human psyche.

  • You love numbers because you know that behind every number there is a story

    Most designers hate numbers. Also, most designers are shit. Coincidence? We think not. Behind every successful product is a designer who when faced with adverse data had the humility to accept the limitation of assumptions and the insight to figure out the next course of action.

  • You like to keep up with the latest developments

    You understand the fundamentals of good design, but also realize that it is a constantly evolving field. Hence, you keep a tab not just on the latest research but also the latest design fads. :)
