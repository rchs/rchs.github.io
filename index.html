How we work

01

Hands on Approach

We aren’t investors and we definitely aren’t technology partners. We take a hands on approach in every company we work with.

02

Partner with Industry Players

No team is complete without people who have industry insight and years of experience. We never start a project unless there is someone with industry expertise on board.

03

Culture of Learning and Problem Solving

Since we work exclusively with in-house products, you will learn a lot about the process of conceptualizing, designing, developing, measuring, and iterating products.

04

Share expertise across companies

The best thing about having multiple technology companies under one roof is that the overlap and differences between them create an environment of support and discovery.
